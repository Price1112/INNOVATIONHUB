ABOUT THE COMPANY

Meyiswa Tranining Academy Meyiswa training academy is a 100% black female owned company,run and owned by a woman who goes by the name Thandile Meyiswa who hails from the rurals of the Easteren Cape.Who believed that education was the only way to a brighter future for her. Meyiswa Training Academy is an accredited learning institution specializing in Training and Learnership initiatives focused on the BBBEE codes of practice.They are driven by an entrepreneurial spirit to ensure that they meet their clients needs and add value to their business. Their goal is to meet and exceed client’s expectations by offering best practices,outstanding services and help companies improve their operational efficiencies. The founder is so qualified in the business industry that she holds a MBA Graduate from Henley Business school as well as a B Tech Degree in Human Resources Management from UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH AFRICA.
